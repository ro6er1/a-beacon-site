A Beacon is a point-and-click adventure set in a dark medieval fantasy world, with humor — actual humor, not the odd giggle buried in 14 hours of fetch quests. I am heavily influenced by Monkey Island, Monty Python and early Blackadder comedy-stylings.

Build wise it is a solo project I'm working on using s&box engine and illustrating everything by hand in Adobe Fresco. It's early days but yeah.

Here is a snippet of game play and some screen shots so far. I don't want to give away too much so here's a sneak-peak. More too come as and when 🪦
